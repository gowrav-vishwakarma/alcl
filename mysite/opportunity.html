Terms used in the Marketing plan


DP        The price at which the product is available to the DISTRIBUTORS
PV        Point value of the product which will be the basis of the calculation in the repurchase plan. Each PV is equal to Rs.5.
            This PV will form the basis for calculation of incentives in all incomes except the 1st and 2nd of the Marketing Plan.
GPV    The sum total of PV in your group/network calculated in your unlimited depth.
PPV     The PV of the personal purchase required at each differential level in the Repurchase plan.
CV       Company volume.
